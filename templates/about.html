About DIY AI Assistant

Our Mission

DIY AI Assistant is dedicated to making home improvement accessible to everyone. We combine the power of artificial intelligence with practical home improvement knowledge to provide personalized, step-by-step guidance for your DIY projects.

What We Offer

Expert Guidance

Get detailed, step-by-step instructions for various home improvement projects, from simple repairs to complex renovations.

Safety First

Receive important safety tips and precautions for each project, ensuring you can work confidently and securely.

Material Lists

Access comprehensive lists of required materials and tools for each project, helping you prepare effectively.

24/7 Availability

Get instant assistance whenever you need it, day or night, without waiting for professional consultation.

How It Works

  1. Ask your home improvement question in plain language
  2. Receive detailed, step-by-step instructions

  5. Ask follow-up questions as needed
